How anomaly detection works
Every cylinder dispensing triggers 7 parallel checks in real time:

Quota check → Has consumer used their monthly allowance?
Aadhar dedup → Is this Aadhar linked to multiple active connections?
Dispense rate → Is this agency dispensing 2σ above their own baseline?
Scan compliance → Is the agency skipping Aadhar OTP verification?
Commercial diversion → Is a domestic consumer receiving at a commercial address?
Time pattern → Is bulk dispensing happening outside business hours?
Cylinder journey → Has this cylinder appeared at multiple locations impossibly fast?
